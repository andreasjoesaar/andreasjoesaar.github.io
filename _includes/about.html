About

I am a freelance audio engineer based in Tallinn, Estonia with an audio kit that can be customised for the needs of your production. I have a BA in Film Arts from the Baltic Film and Media School in Tallinn. Going to a film school means I have been battle-hardened in some of the most difficult scenarios a production could come up against. The approach I use for any scenario is remaining calm and keeping a clear line of communication with the crew.
